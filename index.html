About

From engineering
to knowledge systems

My professional trajectory spans mechanical engineering, over fifteen years of international programme management in the automotive industry across Luxembourg and Portugal, and a transition into public sector digital transformation. This path has shaped a distinctive capacity: designing rigorous, scalable solutions at the intersection of operational discipline and systems thinking.

At Portugal's Secretaria-Geral do Governo, I have led cloud migration initiatives serving more than 200 users across 100+ government entities, and implemented a Balanced Scorecard Ontology using semantic web technologies — the subject of my MSc thesis and the foundation of my ongoing PhD research at ISCTE-IUL.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering (Instituto Superior Técnico), an MBA (ISEG), and a Master's in Public Administration Digitalization (ISCTE-IUL, 18.2/20), complemented by Microsoft Azure certifications in Fabric Analytics and Data Fundamentals.
