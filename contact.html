Repository Access

Requesting access
to private repositories

The public repositories in this portfolio are curated showcases presenting methodology, approach, and results. Full implementation code, internal datasets, production configurations, and SG-Gov strategic indicators are maintained in private repositories.

Access is available to academic and professional collaborators with a legitimate interest in the work. To request access, please connect via LinkedIn with a brief note describing your background and the specific project or research area you are interested in.

What to include in your request. A brief introduction — your role or research background, the project or repository you are interested in, and the purpose of your interest. I aim to respond to all serious enquiries within a few working days.
