Data Science · Machine Learning

Titanic — Machine Learning from Disaster

End-to-end ML pipeline implemented twice — on a traditional local stack (Python, VS Code, SQL Server) and on Microsoft Fabric (Lakehouse, MLflow, Direct Lake). Covers EDA, data cleaning, feature engineering, model comparison (Logistic Regression, Random Forest, Gradient Boosting), and reporting. Best model: Random Forest at 83.1% cross-validation accuracy. Business Intelligence · Data Engineering

Contoso Direct Sales — Self-Service BI

Complete Self-Service BI solution processing 5.4 million sales transactions across 306 stores, implemented twice in parallel: traditional stack (SQL Server, SSIS, Power BI Desktop) and Microsoft Fabric (Lakehouse, PySpark, DirectLake). Star/snowflake schema with 10 tables, 5 hierarchies, row-level security, and fiscal calendar analysis. Side-by-side comparison of on-premises and cloud-native delivery.

Enterprise Architecture · Government Digital Transformation

Process Platform Architecture

Six-layer enterprise process management platform designed for a Portuguese central government agency coordinating financial and budgetary processes across 117+ external entities. Architecture eliminates Power Apps licensing costs for 200+ external users via SharePoint Online staging, establishes Microsoft Dataverse as the transactional core, separates analytical workloads into Microsoft Fabric/OneLake, and integrates a RAG/AI layer grounded in structured data, indexed documents, and a Balanced Scorecard Ontology knowledge graph — the direct operational application of the MSc research.
